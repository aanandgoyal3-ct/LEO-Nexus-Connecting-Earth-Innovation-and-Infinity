Scalability Roadmap — How to move from idea to reality

Phase 0 — Today (Demo)

  • Use small satellites to test deorbit sails, docking ports and AI software.
  • Run student payloads and small experiments on existing platforms like ISS (or commercial labs).

Phase 1 — Prototype & Service

  • Launch prototype modular modules and a servicing demonstration vehicle.
  • Test in-orbit refueling, swapping modules, and safe deorbit of parts.

Phase 2 — Commercial Operations

  • Private stations host research, manufacturing, and tourism — NASA and others buy services.
  • Implement routine debris removal plans and service networks.

How this improves present systems

  • Reduces long-term cost by sharing infrastructure with many customers.
  • Encourages faster tech development because private companies compete to build better modules.
  • Creates more jobs and research opportunities on Earth and in orbit.
